Total assumed costs: 6.0 bps (fees + spread + slippage)

Problem

Prices can diverge briefly across venues. The challenge is filtering out non-executable “mirage” signals once realistic costs and liquidity are applied.

Approach

  • Normalize symbols and align timestamps across sources.
  • Compute effective buy/sell prices with fees + spread + slippage assumptions.
  • Filter by liquidity/volume and execution constraints.
  • Log snapshots + signals for evaluation and backtesting.

Architecture

Baseline liquidity: bid–ask spread distribution across venues (zoomed 0–6 bps).
